Methodology & Sources

Each column is a single enactment roll call. Several taxes rode inside one budget bill, so a Yea is a vote for the whole package — the column lists every increase it enacted. A member can't have split their vote between taxes in the same bill.

Every mark is transcribed from the official Maine Legislature roll-call record and the parsed counts were verified to reconcile exactly to the printed tallies. Rosters differ between the June 2025 and April 2026 votes; members not seated for a given vote are left blank, never counted as “No.”
